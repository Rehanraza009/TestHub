About TestHub

TestHub is a comprehensive online examination platform designed for schools, colleges, coaching institutes, and placement tests. Our platform provides a seamless experience for both educators and students.

With TestHub, educators can easily create and manage question banks, conduct timed exams, and automatically evaluate student responses. Students can access exams from anywhere, get instant results, and track their performance over time.
